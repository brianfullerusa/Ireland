O'Connor's Traditional Pub

Killarney activity review • Thursday 9/3 night

Snapshot

O'Connor's is a family-run traditional pub on High Street in Killarney with a strong reputation for authentic Irish music sessions. Unlike some of the larger, more tourist-oriented pubs in town, O'Connor's has maintained a genuinely local feel — the kind of place where the musicians play because they want to, the regulars have their usual seats, and the atmosphere builds naturally through the evening rather than being manufactured. The pub has been in the same family for generations and the commitment to traditional music is woven into its identity. Sessions happen nightly, and the quality is consistently high. For visitors who want to experience Killarney's music scene at its most authentic, O'Connor's is the Thursday night destination.

Why it made the list

  • Family-run with deep roots in traditional music — the sessions are genuine, not tourist shows.
  • Less touristy than some other Killarney music pubs — attracts locals and serious music lovers.
  • High Street location makes it a natural after-dinner stop from Treyvaud's — just steps away.
  • The music is consistently excellent — O'Connor's has earned a strong reputation among trad musicians.
  • A different experience from Wednesday's Courtney's or Danny Mann — Thursday brings a fresh pub perspective.

Common review themes

What reviewers love

  • "The most authentic music session we experienced in Ireland" — the quality and genuineness are praised.
  • The family atmosphere is welcoming — visitors feel like guests rather than customers.
  • The pub retains its character despite Killarney's tourism — "it hasn't sold out."
  • Good mix of locals and visitors creates a balanced, warm atmosphere.

Common complaints

  • Can fill up quickly on good music nights — arrive by 9:00 for the best seats.
  • Limited food options — not the place for a meal.
  • The traditional sessions may be unfamiliar to those expecting popular Irish singalongs.
  • Smaller than some pubs — a large group may not all sit together.

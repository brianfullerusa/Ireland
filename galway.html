Galway & the West

The wild Atlantic edge

After picking up two rental cars in Dublin on Monday morning, the group drives west on the M6 (~2.5 hours) to Galway. Two nights on the west coast gives time for the Cliffs of Moher, the Burren, Connemara or the Aran Islands, plus an evening in Galway itself — traditional music on Shop Street, seafood on the docks, and the famous Galway pub scene.

Mon Aug 31 – Wed Sep 2, 2026

Galway & the West

The wild Atlantic edge

Planning in progress

After picking up two rental cars in Dublin on Monday morning, the group drives west on the M6 (~2.5 hours) to Galway. Two nights on the west coast gives time for the Cliffs of Moher, the Burren, Connemara or the Aran Islands, plus an evening in Galway itself — traditional music on Shop Street, seafood on the docks, and the famous Galway pub scene.

  • Drive Dublin → Galway via the M6 (~2.5 hours)
  • Cliffs of Moher + the Burren day
  • Connemara or the Aran Islands (Inis Mór)
  • Galway city evening: trad music + seafood

🚙 Pick up 2 rental cars in Dublin Monday AM, depart by 10:00 to beat traffic.

Killarney & Kerry

Lakes, mountains and the Ring

Activities ready

  • Alternatives: Ring of Kerry drive or Gap of Dunloe

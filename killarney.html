Killarney & Kerry

Lakes, mountains and the Ring

South to Kerry for the scenic heart of the trip. Base in Killarney for three nights. The obvious must-dos are the Ring of Kerry drive, Killarney National Park (Muckross House, Torc Waterfall, Ladies' View) and the Gap of Dunloe by jaunting car. Dingle makes a strong alternative or a side day. Evenings: music and pub food in Killarney town.

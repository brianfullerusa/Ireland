The recommended plan

Dublin — Three Day Itinerary

Based on the travel agent panel’s recommendations across all twelve activities.

Saturday

August 29 — Arrival

11:45 AM

Land at Dublin Airport

EI 58 from Denver arrives 11:45 AM. Allow 45–60 min for immigration and baggage.

1:30 PM

Check in at Trinity City Hotel

Pre-booked minivan transfer. Hotel will have rooms ready for an early check-in.

2:30 PM

Late lunch near the hotel

Fallon & Byrne deli, Avoca Café, or the Bank on College Green.

4:00 PM

Chester Beatty Library

Free, quiet, fully accessible. Perfect jet-lag afternoon. 8-min walk from hotel, 90 minutes inside. Skip the castle tour this trip.

6:30 PM

The Brazen Head — Dinner

First pub dinner of the trip. Book early slot, order safe classics, leave by 8:30–9:00 PM. Don’t wait for the 9:30 PM music on arrival night.

Sunday

August 30 — Full Day

9:30 AM

Trinity College & Book of Kells

Student-led tour bundles campus walk with Book of Kells entry. Enter via Nassau Street for a quieter approach. Book now.

11:30 AM

Guinness Storehouse

Book 11:30 AM entry + 1:30 PM lunch at the 1837 Brasserie as a combined package. Gravity Bar at the top.

2:30 PM

Hotel rest

Essential block. 60–90 minutes. Parents definitely need it; everyone benefits.

3:30 PM

Irish Whiskey Museum (Premium tour)

3-minute walk from hotel. 4-whiskey comparative tasting, 60 minutes. Best whiskey option for this group.

5:45 PM

Dinner at The Pig’s Ear

Early dinner before the pub crawl. Overlooks Trinity. Elevator-accessible upstairs room.

7:00 PM

Dublin Literary Pub Crawl (4 adults)

Meet at The Duke at 7:00 PM (tour starts 7:30). Ends ~9:45 PM. Parents have their own quiet dinner and turn in.

Monday

August 31 — Departure

8:00 AM

Leisurely breakfast, pack up

Check out of Trinity City.

9:30 AM

Kilmainham Gaol Optional — book 28 days ahead

If booked: 09:30 tour, 90 min inside, then airport car pickup en route west. Email for accessible tour arrangement for the parents.

11:00 AM

Collect the rental car

7-seat minivan, automatic. Pickup at Dublin Airport if doing Kilmainham, or city-center office otherwise.

Noon

Depart Dublin for Galway

~2.5 hours via the M6.

Advance-booking checklist

  • Trinity College & Book of Kells — student-led tour, Sunday 9:30 AM
  • Guinness Storehouse — Sunday 11:30 AM + 1837 Brasserie 1:30 PM
  • Irish Whiskey Museum — Premium tour, Sunday 3:30 PM
  • Dublin Literary Pub Crawl — Sunday 7:30 PM (4 tickets direct at dublinpubcrawl.com)
  • The Brazen Head — Saturday 6:30 PM via OpenTable
  • The Pig’s Ear — Sunday 5:45 PM
  • Kilmainham Gaol — 28 days before Monday 8/31 (set alarm for August 1–2 midnight Irish time)
  • Airport arrival minivan transfer — DUB to Trinity City, Saturday 8/29
  • Rental car — 7-seat minivan, automatic, lock in 90+ days out
  • 9/5 return hotel — Clayton Dublin Airport recommended
